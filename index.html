Project Shining Knight

About

Project Shining Knight is an initiative by Venture Scouts from Dunman High School and Ahmad Ibrahim Secondary School. Aiming to tackle the issue on house hoarding, we have partnered with Keeping Hope Alive to enhance their own knowledge, and to educate the public on this unseen problem that is always happening around us.

Who this is for

Households who have a large excess of miscellaneous items stored in the corridor, and those experiencing house hoarding.

How you can help

We are seeking donations of cleaning supplies for the targeted households that we are cleaning. We are also seeking volunteers to help out with the cleaning!

Donation Drive Date: 15 January 2024 to 28 February 2024
